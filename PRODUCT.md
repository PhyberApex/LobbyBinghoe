# Product

## Register

product

## Users

LobbyHoes: fans of the German podcast "Hobbylos" by Rezo and Julien Bäm. They open the app alongside a live stream or episode, one hand free, attention split. They glance at the bingo card between moments rather than staring at it. The session is social — someone might be playing competitively with friends on separate devices, comparing progress. The mood is chaotic, affectionate, communal.

## Product Purpose

LobbyBinghoe lets fans play bingo live during Hobbylos episodes. Success looks like: user gets on the board in under 30 seconds, marks squares without thinking, and feels the genuine thrill of yelling "BINGO" at their screen. The app should feel like it was built by a fan who loves the podcast — not a product team.

## Brand Personality

Fan-made, chaotic, affectionate. The energy of a Discord server, not a startup homepage.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS/startup: no gradient heroes, no "hero metric" card grids, no Tailwind-blue
- Off-the-shelf Material Design / Quasar defaults: needs personality on top, not raw component library output
- Casino/gambling apps: no neon-on-dark gambling aesthetic
- Kids games: not childish or cartoonish — these are adults being extremely online

## Design Principles

1. **Glanceable first.** Core game state (which squares are marked, how close to bingo) must read in one glance. Users are half-watching something else.
2. **Fan energy over polish.** This should feel made with love, not manufactured. Specific, opinionated details signal "a fan made this."
3. **Chaos with a skeleton.** Personality lives in the details, but the layout holds. The grid is sacred; the chrome can be weird.
4. **Celebration earns its moment.** Animations and feedback land on real events (mark a square, get a bingo), not as ambient decoration.
5. **Community signal.** Every design choice should reinforce that this is for a specific community — breadth of appeal is not the goal.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific requirements. Make it usable: readable contrast, tap targets that work on mobile, core functionality reachable without hover.
